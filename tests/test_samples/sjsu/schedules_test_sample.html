Summer 2025 Class Schedule

There are three different types of modes of instruction: Fully Online, Hybrid, and In-Person (definitions linked).  Some instruction modes and classes times may change before registration opens. In addition to offering classes, students can also explore Study and Abroad Programs.

Calendars

Current Students | Open University Students

Summer 2025 Culminating Activities & Final Exams are normally scheduled on the last day of class. SJSU does not offer winter term commencement ceremonies.

If the below table is not loading, please visit the MySJSU Class Schedule Search.

  • Online Learning Readiness
    • Before enrolling in an online course, students should first assess their readiness for stepping into the online learning environment by completing the Online Learning Readiness Questionnaire.
  • Using the Search

    • Use the Search bar to filter by section and/or mode of instruction (e.g. typing "Fully Online MATH" will provide a list of all MATH courses taught fully online.)
